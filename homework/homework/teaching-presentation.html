Why Contrast?

“Contrast creates excitement and visual appeal that helps your audience focus on what’s important.”

What?

  • Size
  • Color
  • Value
  • Typefaces
  • Shapes

Examples

Color: The single green apple stands out against the green apple. In the image of all green apples nothing stands out.

Shape:All squares would have been boring, the star adds excitement and diversity.

Value: The dark and light colors make an image with contrast.

Size: The eye goes to the one small circle.

Typefaces: Having a sans-serif and serif font creates contrast. Its important to have no more than two different fonts.

Website Example

This website is a great examples of contrast. It has great color contrast between the blue elements and the white type. There is a value contrast when hovering over links. There is size contast between elements.


One sentence take away: When everything is important, nothing is important!
