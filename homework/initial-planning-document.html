Purpose Statement

Providing reliable weather forecasts to allow our users to best prepare against everyday weather.

Audience

Users range from early twenties to later fifties. They are everyday people who are constantly on the go, and want to be prepared about the weather. They live in the city and in the outskirts of town. They are busy with school, work, and taking care of their families. They access this website multiple times a day, using whatever form of technology they currently have available. There is a wide range of levels of income for users, as all classes of income are affected and need to adapt to the days weather conditions.

Persona

Meet Sara Brown

  • Mother and Caretaker
  • 37 years old
  • Sara is married with three beautiful daughters. She has her Bachelors in Family Science and is an online blogger.
  • Her goals include: Being a loving and nurturing mother. She does this by being prepared each day to take care of her family. She wants to prepare them in everyway she can, which includes knowing each day how to properly dress for the weather.
  • Each morning she wakes up and checks the weather using her Iphone. During the day she will occasionally check the weather using her laptop.
  • Quote: "My family is the most important thing, and I have a responsibility to take care of them. That includes being aware of the weather and dailyweather.com allows me to do that easily."
